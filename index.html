Your Portal to the Best Stories

Explore some of the best and most popular light novels. Curated for fans, updated frequently, and beautifully organized.

Solo Leveling

Follow Sung Jin-Woo, the world's weakest hunter, as he gains powers through a mysterious system and climbs the ranks of the strongest hunters.

Overlord

Trapped in a virtual game as the powerful skeletal wizard Ainz, a player navigates a new reality full of magic and conquest.

Re:Zero

Subaru Natsuki is transported to a fantasy world where he discovers he has the power to rewind time by dying, reliving painful loops to protect those he cares about.

Mushoku Tensei

After dying in his previous life, a hopeless man is reborn in a magical world with the determination to live a better life and make the most of his second chance.

Shield Hero

Naofumi is summoned to another world as the Shield Hero, only to be betrayed. He must now rise against all odds and protect the kingdom with only a shield.

No Game No Life

Siblings Sora and Shiro, undefeated gamers, are transported to a world where all conflicts are settled through games, and they aim to beat the god of this world.

Reincarnated as a Slime

After dying, a salaryman is reborn in a fantasy world as a slime with unique powers, and starts building a diverse nation of monsters and allies.

Eminence in Shadow

Obsessed with becoming a shadowy mastermind, Cid is reincarnated in another world where he unknowingly builds a secret organization fighting a real evil cult.

Classroom of the Elite

In an elite high school where competition is everything, Kiyotaka Ayanokōji hides his true intellect while manipulating his way through social and academic challenges.

Sword Art Online

Kirito and thousands of players are trapped in a VRMMO where dying in-game means dying in real life. He must fight his way through to escape the virtual prison.
