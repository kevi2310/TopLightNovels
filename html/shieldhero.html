Shield Hero

From betrayal to redemption with only a shield.

About the Novel

Author: Aneko Yusagi

Genre: Isekai, Action, Fantasy, Drama

Status: Ongoing

Naofumi Iwatani is summoned to another world as the Shield Hero. Betrayed and left alone, he must rise from the bottom using only defense and determination to fight for justice and reclaim his name.
