Solo Leveling

The legendary rise of the world's weakest hunter.

About the Novel

Author: Chu-Gong

Genre: Action, Fantasy, System, Dungeon

Status: Completed (270+ chapters)

In a world where hunters—human warriors with supernatural abilities—must battle deadly monsters to protect humanity, Sung Jin-Woo finds himself as the weakest hunter. But when he awakens a mysterious system that lets him level up, his journey to become the strongest begins.
